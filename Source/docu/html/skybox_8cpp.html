vertexShaderSource is the vertex shader for the skybox

vertex shader will only draw two triangles, limited to the part of the screen that can be affected. The vertex input is 0,0 in one corner and 1,1 in the other. Draw the quad at z -1, with x and y going from -1 to 1 (and then transformed with the model matrix). todo 2.
